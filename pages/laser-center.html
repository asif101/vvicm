ClearV™
Spider Vein Treatment

ClearV™ is the preferred choice for vascular treatments since it effectively and safely treats visible vessels, broken capillaries, spider veins, vascular lesions and skin discoloration. 1064nm has strong absorption in oxyhemoglobin providing selective heating of veins to eliminate them and make unwanted vessels less noticeable.
