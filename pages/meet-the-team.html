Meet the Team
Board certified and dedicated to providing medical care specifically tailored to your needs
Vincent Noori, M.D., RPVI
Paul Lucas, M.D.
Alain Tanbe, M.D., RPVI
Doctors Lucas, Tanbe, and Noori of VVICM are board certified vascular surgeons dedicated to the comprehensive treatment of all facets of vascular disease. Our providers are leading experts in the field.

Dr. Noori is a Board Certified Vascular & Endovascular Surgeon. He is also a Registered Physician in Vascular Interpretation (RPVI). He completed his vascular surgical residency training at Maine Medical Center-Tufts University School of Medicine.

Dr. Noori covers the full spectrum of vascular care including peripheral artery disease, vascular blockages, lower extremities circulation problems, leg swelling, leg pain, varicose veins, spider veins, venous insufficiency, diabetic wounds, dialysis access, carotid artery disease and vascular aneurysms. His areas of interest include minimally invasive vascular surgery, for which he utilizes the latest and most cutting-edge minimally invasive equipment and techniques.

Dr. Noori works closely with every individual patient to assure the most appropriate treatment of their vascular issues. He provides all of his patients with compassionate care and aims to build stronger doctor-patient relationships.

Dr. Lucas is a Board Certified Vascular Surgeon with nearly 20 years of experience in private practice. He specializes and treats a multitude of vascular diseases and conditions, both arterial and venous, using minimally invasive and open techniques to ensure the best possible outcomes for his patients.

Dr. Lucas has been frequently recognized as a Baltimore Magazine Top Doc, and is known for his compassionate and caring doctor-patient relationships, and believes that medical care is a comprehensive team effort.

Dr. Tanbe is a fellowship-trained, Board Certified Vascular Surgeon who serves as the Medical Director of the Vein and Vascular Institute of Central Maryland (VVICM). His surgical residency was completed at Maine Medical Center-Tufts University and he performed a vascular fellowship at North Shore University hospital in New York.

Dr. Tanbe covers the full spectrum of vascular care including peripheral artery disease, vascular blockages, lower extremities circulation problems, leg swelling, Varicose veins, spider veins, Venous insufficiency, diabetic wounds, Carotid artery disease and vascular aneurysms.

He utilizes the latest and most cutting-edge minimally invasive equipment and techniques and works closely with every patient to assure the most appropriate treatment of their vascular issues.

Paul Lucas, M.D.

Dr. Lucas is a vascular surgeon with nearly 20 years of experience in private practice. He serves as the Medical Director of the Vein and Vascular Institute of Central Maryland (VVICM), and is a Board Certified Vascular Surgeon. He specializes and treats a multitude of vascular diseases and conditions, both arterial and venous, using minimally invasive and open techniques to ensure the best possible outcomes for his patients.

Dr Lucas has a special interest in spider veins, and is a trained laser specialist offering multiple modalities for treatment. He is the only vascular surgeon in the region to offer Veingogh and Sciton laser treatments with ClearV.

In addition, Dr Lucas offers laser skin care using HALO, BBL, and skin resurfacing peels to reduce wrinkles and fine lines, and to provide his patients with a more rejuvenated appearance.

Dr Lucas has been frequently recognized as a Baltimore Magazine Top Doc, and is known for his compassionate and caring doctor-patient relationships, and believes that medical care is a comprehensive team effort.
